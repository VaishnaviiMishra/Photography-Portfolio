Bit About Me

I've been passionate about photography since 10th grade, but my skills truly skyrocketed when I entered college and joined the photography society. Immersing myself in this community, I learned a great deal and decided to invest in my craft by buying my first camera on August 14, 2023. I quickly mastered it, and soon my photos improved tenfold. This growth inspired me to start freelancing as a photographer, primarily shooting on weekends. As an engineering student, balancing both my studies and my love for photography has been challenging, but I'm determined to keep this passion alive and will never let it go. I'm also proficient in Lightroom, which allows me to produce stunning editing results that enhance the beauty of my work.
